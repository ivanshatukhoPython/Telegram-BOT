Command List:

/add - Add an account to the bot.
Format:
/add account password
If you have two-factor enabled, the bot will prompt you to send the code.

/newtarget - Change like target.
Format:
/newtarget account:target

You can have one target for every account you have registered.

/listaccounts - List accounts/targets you have registered the bot (and are currently authenticated).
Format:
/listaccounts

/droptype - Change account drop type.
Format:
/droptype account:slow/fast

Change drop type to/from burst mode (likes all at once) or slow (likes over 15 minutes). Note: 'account' is the account you have registered with the bot, not your target.

/remove - Remove an account.
Format:
/remove account
